How to use

SignUp

1. Open the college faculty rating website in your preferred web browser.

2. Look for the "Sign Up" or "Create an Account" option on the website's homepage.

3. Click on the appropriate link and provide the required information, such as your name, email address, and a unique username/password combination.

4. Follow the instructions provided to complete the sign-up process. Some websites may require email verification, so check your email and follow the verification link if necessary.

Search for faculty

1. After signing up and logging in to the website, you will typically be directed to the homepage or a search page.

2. Locate the search bar, usually at the top of the page, and click on it to activate..

3. Enter the name of the faculty member you wish to search for. You can also search by course name or department to find specific professors teaching a particular subject.

4. Once you have entered the search criteria, click on the "Search" or "Find" button to initiate the search process.

Rating Faculty

1. After searching for the faculty member, you should see their profile or a list of relevant results.

2. Click on the desired faculty member's name to access their profile page.

3. On the profile page, you will usually find information about the faculty member, such as their name, department, courses taught, and previous ratings.

4. Locate the option to rate the faculty member represented by stars.

5. Consider your personal experience with the faculty member and choose the appropriate rating. Higher ratings generally indicate positive experiences, while lower ratings reflect less satisfactory experiences.

6. After selecting the rating, make sure to provide any additional comments or feedback in the designated text box. This can be helpful for other students seeking more detailed insights.

7. Once you have given the faculty member a rating and written any comments, click on the "Submit" or "Save" button to save your rating and contribute it to the website.
