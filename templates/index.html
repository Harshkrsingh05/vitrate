Welcome to VITRATE!

Here you can rate your faculties.

We understand that choosing the right courses and professors can be a daunting task for students. That's why we've created a platform for you to rate and review your professors and faculties at VIT Chennai. Our goal is to provide a space where students can share their experiences and help each other make informed decisions when it comes to their education.

By using our website, you'll have access to a wealth of information about professors and faculties, including ratings, reviews, and feedback from your peers. Whether you're a new student looking to choose your courses, or a current student seeking to provide feedback on a recent course, our website is here to help. So start exploring, leave your reviews, and join the community today!

How Our Rating System Works!

Our rating system is designed to provide a fair and objective assessment of each professor and faculty member at VIT Chennai.

When leaving a review, you will be asked to rate your professor or faculty member on a scale of 1 to 5, with 1 being the lowest and 5 being the highest. You can rate based on factors such as teaching style, course content, accessibility, and more.
